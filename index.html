Welcome to Threads + Stones

Created by two girls from California, Threads + Stones is a collection of handmade art, thrifted clothing and decor. Our mission is to create upcycled art unique to California that inspires others

  • Custom Jewerly

    Our jewerly collection includes bracelets, necklaces, rings and anklets.

  • Macrame

    Macrame includes home decor wall hangs and handmade jewerly out of hemp threads.

  • Upcycled Denim

    We create new pieces out of vintage finds! Denim can be personalized, painted and embroidered.
